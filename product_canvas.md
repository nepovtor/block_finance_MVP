# Block Finance Product Canvas

## One-line pitch

Block Finance is a youth banking concept where card activity instantly unlocks game rewards, making spending, saving, and return engagement feel visible and fun.

## User problem

- Banking apps feel passive and low-motivation for younger users
- Rewards are often delayed, invisible, or too abstract
- Saving goals and referral loops are rarely tied into one emotional product story

## Product promise

- Spend: a real card action creates an immediate response
- Reward: the user gets a visible gameplay advantage
- Progress: challenge, savings, XP, and social proof all reinforce the loop

## MVP layers in this repo

- Onboarding explains the loop in under 8 seconds
- Dashboard shows profile, reward, daily challenge, savings goal, referral, and analytics pulse
- Payment flow grants an `extra_move` reward through the existing backend
- Puzzle game consumes the reward as a real gameplay boost

## Why a bank should care

- Encourages repeat card usage with visible habit mechanics
- Adds a savings motivation surface without heavy financial complexity
- Creates a shareable, youth-friendly product story
- Makes engagement measurable with lightweight analytics events

## Why a user should care

- Banking actions feel immediately rewarding
- Progress is visible right away
- Saving and daily activity feel game-like, not bureaucratic
- The product is simple enough to understand in one short session

## Demo proof points

- One tap payment lights up a reward card
- The same payment advances a visible challenge
- Savings goal can be topped up live in demo mode
- Referral CTA simulates viral growth
- Analytics events are visible and easy to explain
- Game reward can be used in front of judges

## Next step after hackathon

- Replace local demo state with backend-backed challenge/savings/referral data
- Add real referral attribution and social share generation
- Tune reward economy and segmentation by behavior
